System Capabilities

Fixing the flaws of traditional, paper-based municipalities.

Geo-Tagged Issue Routing

Citizens pinpoint issues like potholes or water leaks with sub-meter accuracy via Live Maps. The system automatically routes the ticket to the specific Ward Department responsible for resolution.

Smart Sifarish

End the long lines at the ward. Digitize the application, tracking, and signing of official recommendation letters.

AI Guidance Chatbot

Navigate complex civic processes instantly. Our AI Chatbot provides immediate answers to municipal regulations in both English and Nepali.

Two-Way Transparency Dashboard

Unlike existing systems which focus solely on documents, CivicSewa creates a two-way street. Citizens track municipal response times, and admins get a real-time heat map of local issues.
